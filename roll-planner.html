Trade Counter // Carpet Tool

Roll Planner

Work out linear metres, seams and waste for rectangular rooms, L-shapes, and stairs & landings — before you cut.
Planning aid, not a template. Carpet runs one direction only (pile/pattern match), so strips can't be rotated to cut waste like tiles. Stairs use an adjustable per-step allowance — always confirm awkward staircases with an on-site measure before ordering.
Strips are cut to the room's longest run, matched across seams. Landing/stair widths are cut from the same roll width.
